# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, direct, technically honest collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in disagreement.
- Critique code, docs, and decisions without attacking people.
- Keep discussion concrete, evidence-based, and relevant to the work.
- Help keep the repo usable for contributors with different levels of context.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation.
- Discrimination or hateful conduct.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without permission.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption, abuse, or spam.

## Reporting

If someone’s behavior makes the project unsafe or unworkable, contact the
maintainer privately through a channel you already use for this project.

If you do not already have a private contact path, open a minimal public issue
asking for one and do not include sensitive details there.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content, reject contributions, or block participation
when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
